# App Features

## Authentication & User Management
- **Login & Signup**: Secure user authentication.
- **Password Management**: Forgot password, reset password, and change password functionality.
- **User Profile**: View and edit user profile details.

## Content Discovery
- **Home Page**: Dashboard with banners, categories, and quick access links.
- **Search**: Global search functionality to find content across the app.
- **Notifications**: System notifications to keep users updated.
- **Country Selection**: Support for multiple countries/regions with specific content.

## Business Directory
- **Business Listings**: Browse a directory of businesses.
- **Filtering**: Filter businesses by category and location.
- **Business Details**: Comprehensive business information including:
    - Contact details (phone, email, website).
    - Opening hours.
    - Photo gallery.
    - Menu links.
    - Ratings and reviews.

## Events
- **Event Listings**: View upcoming and past events.
- **Event Details**: Detailed information about events, including date, location, and organizer.

## Jobs
- **Job Board**: Browse available job openings.
- **Job Details**: View job descriptions, requirements, and company information.

## News & Updates
- **News Articles**: Read the latest news and updates.
- **News Details**: Full article view with images and content.

## Offers & Promotions
- **Special Offers**: Browse special deals and discounts.
- **Offer Filtering**: Filter offers by category.
- **Banners**: Promotional banners on the home page and other sections.

## Dynamic Content (Hub)
- **Hub Sections**: Configurable content sections that can be displayed as lists, grids, or rows.
- **Emergency Contacts**: Special layout for emergency numbers.

## Legal & Support
- **Legal Documents**: Access terms of service, privacy policy, and other legal documents.
- **Support**: Access support information and FAQs.
